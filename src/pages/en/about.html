About KJ's Campfire

KJ's Campfire is a small, family‑run campground on the sunny southern coast of Sicily. We started with a single idea: share the magic of crackling fires, open skies, and Sicilian hospitality with fellow travelers.

Whether you arrive by motorcycle, camper van, or backpack, you'll find tucked‑away pitches among the olive trees, nightly campfire jams, and the scent of orange blossoms drifting on the breeze.

Stay a night or settle in for a season—either way, you'll leave with sand in your shoes and stories for the road.
